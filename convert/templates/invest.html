财表上必看资料

Section Condition Meaning
Income Statement
Revenue -Positive
-Increase/Constant		 Revenue increases means growing company.
Net Income(Adj.) -Positive
-Increase or Constant or Decrease with reason		 Net Income(Adj.) means the net profit after taxes and liabities. It should be increases mainly but in range of two years decreases with reason like develop a big project is acceptable.
Gross Profit Margin -Positive
-Increase		 Gross Profit Margin means a ratio of profit of sales increases after operating cost.
Net Profit Margin -Positive
-Increase		 Net Profit Margin means the level of profit from sales.
Return On Equity(ROE) -Positive
-Increase
->15%		 Return On Equity means return on shareholders' equity(身家) and reflect the company generate net profit by net assets.
Diluted EPS(Adj.) -Positive
-Increase		 Diluted Earning Per Share means the profit per share after taxes.
Balance Sheet
Total Debt to Equity Ratio -Normal< 0.5x
-Danger>= 0.5x
-Bankruptcy> 1.5x		 Total Debt to Equity Ratio means the equity and debt relationship.
Net Debt(Cash) to Equity -Negative
-Decrease		 Net Debt(Cash) to Equity means the company net debt. So the least, the best.
CAPEX -Negative in two year
-Profit Increase		 CAPEX means the company expand their business. Company can be negative in range of two years but profit after the year should grow. Profit grow means the expand is worth.
Cash from Operations -Positive
-Increase		 Cash from Operations means the amount of cash of a company from carrying out operation like generate revenue,paying expenses and funding working capital over a period of time.
Cash Flow from Financing -Negative
-Decrease		 Cash flow from financing means the amount of cash of a company from carrying out financing activities include transactions involving debt, equity, and dividends. Negative means clear the debt, give dividend etc and is good;
Cash Flow from Investing -Positive
-Increase		 Cash flow from investing means the amount of cash of a company from carrying out investing activities include purchases of physical assets, investments in securities, or the sale of securities or assets. Negative is bad and mean poor performance.
Free Cash Flow (FCF) -Positive
-Increase		 Free cash flow (FCF) means the cash a company generates after accounting for cash outflows to support operations and maintain its capital assets.
